What is thparq?

A tiny helper library for DOM manipulation that brings back jQuery-like syntax without the bloat!

Why should I use it?

If you miss jQuery's simplicity but want to stay vanilla, thparq gives you the best of both worlds!

How big is it?

Super tiny! The minified version is just a few KB, perfect for modern web development.
